About Pezzoni Engineering

Pezzoni Engineering is a progressive electrical engineering firm dedicated to providing our clients with high quality and timely solutions to difficult problems. We offer a complete range of electrical engineering services from the preliminary study phase through project construction administration. Our specialties include power systems, lighting, information technology, communications, security, fire alarm and controls systems design, energy management and value engineering services. We have 29 years of project experience across a thorough cross-section of the industry. Browse our projects resume for more examples from our extensive project list.

Our staff is made up of highly skilled professional and sub-professional personnel with many years of combined engineering experience and a deep commitment to client satisfaction and engineering excellence. Current staff includes four registered professional electrical engineers overseeing an array of skilled design personnel in three full service offices across California. Our competent and practical team of engineers, designers, CAD operators and administrative personnel work together to provide full service engineering solutions suited to specific client and project needs, consistently delivered on time every time.
